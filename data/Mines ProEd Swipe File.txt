About ProEd
Mines ProEd delivers flexible, professional education options from Colorado School of Mines. Our courses translate cutting-edge technical expertise into real-world business impact.


•	Gain fluency in the science and economics driving energy and resource industries
•	Learn directly from experts shaping today’s technologies
•	Build credibility with engineers, investors, and clients
•	Discover opportunities for innovation, sustainability, and growth
•	Step into new responsibility, accelerate advancement, or career pivot with new technical expertise

—

Of the many things that differentiate Colorado School of Mines from other universities, is our long history of preparing industry-ready graduates that go on to successful careers and industry leadership roles. That hasn't happened by accident. It reflects the strong partnerships that we have with industry and our commitment to aligning our education with industry needs. 

That’s why we’re pleased to offer Mines ProEd, our professional education platform designed specifically to meet the evolving needs of the workforce and industry.

Whether you’re looking to sharpen your technical skills, expand your leadership capacity, or pivot to a new career path, Mines ProEd offers flexible learning formats—from self-paced online modules to in-person sessions—so you can learn in the way that works best for you.

Dedicated Email 1 (Intro to ProEd)

Mines Expertise. Your Next Step Starts Here.
Your career doesn’t stand still. Neither should your skills. Technology, innovation, and global challenges are reshaping industries. To thrive, professionals need to keep learning, growing, and adapting.

That’s why Colorado School of Mines created Mines ProEd: online, hybrid, and in-person courses to help you stay ahead, sharpen your skills, and continue growing in your career.

From mining and sustainability to robotics, data science, leadership, and workplace skills, Mines ProEd courses are designed for working professionals who want to:
•	Gain practical knowledge they can apply immediately
•	Learn from Mines’ world-class faculty and industry experts
•	Fit education into their busy schedules

If you’re ready to invest in your career and future, Mines ProEd is here to guide the way with courses that help you stay ahead in today’s fast-evolving landscape.
